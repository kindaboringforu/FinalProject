《🎈🎈🎈If you are someone curious about the Chinese Culture and excited to learn Chinese, choosing one of theses Scholarships may be the best option for you! Down below you can check it out 😉》 More info

  • A. Scholarship for Four-Week Study

    📌The program commences either in July or December 2019, and provides a four-week scholarship. Applicants holding the X1 or X2 visa are not eligible. An HSK test score is required. Inside this programme you can choose among these 2 options: Traditional Chinese Medicine and Taiji Culture; Chinese Language plus Home-Stay Experience in a Chinese Family.

  • B. Scholarship for One-Semester Study

    📌The program commences either in September 2019 or March 2020, and provides scholarship of a maximum of 5 months. Applicants holding the X1 or X2 visa are not eligible. Applicants shall have a minimum score of 180 on the HSK test (Level 3), and an HSKK test score is required. You can choose between TCSOL, Chinese Language and Literature, Chinese History and Chinese Philosophy or Traditional Chinese Medicine and Taiji Culture.

  • C. Scholarship for Bachelor’s Degree in Teaching Chinese to Speakers of Other Languages (BTCSOL)

    📌The program commences in September 2019 and provides scholarship for a maximum of four academic years. Applicants shall hold a high school diploma and a minimum score of 210 on the HSK Test (Level 4) as well as 60 on the HSKK test (Intermediate Level).
